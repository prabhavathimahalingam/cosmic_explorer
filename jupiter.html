Educational Content

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system, a massive gas giant composed primarily of hydrogen and helium. It has a prominent set of rings, dozens of moons, and a powerful magnetic field.

Historical Timeline

Key events in Jupiter exploration:

  • 1973: Pioneer 10 flyby.
  • 1974: Pioneer 11 flyby.
  • 1979: Voyager 1 and Voyager 2 flybys.
  • 1995: Galileo orbiter arrival.
  • 2016: Juno mission enters orbit.
